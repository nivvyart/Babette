Story

Set within the heart of the iconic Queen Victoria Building in Sydney, Babette is a café that celebrates a contemporary approach to French flavours. Our breakfast and lunch menus honour the best ingredients the season has to offer, alongside an array of artisanal pastries and specialty coffee by multi-winning roaster, ONA.
